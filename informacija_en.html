INFORMATION

Looking for volunteers! Kitchen and bar staff, workshop/entertainment coordinators, people for physical work. If you'd like to join, contact us on FB or prieblanda@protonmail.com.

Location

Preliminary location - Ignalina district. Specified location will be sent to you after ticket/reservation confirmation

Tickets

Tickets can be purchased here - TICKETS

Ticket types:

  1. Standard ticket - 30 Eur (if you'd like to provide more support for the festival, you can purchase a ticket for a higher price);
  2. Reservation - if you haven't made a decision to participate (or there's no tickets left available), you are able to book a reservation allowing you to buy a ticket at the entrance. Possibly, ticket price will increase on the day of the event. To ensure safety people without a ticket will not be allowed inside the festival area.
To ensure safety people without a ticket will not be allowed inside the festival area.

Water

We urge you to take care of water supply by yourselves, respectively. At a distance of 4-5km from festival area you can find a spring. You can reach it by foot as well as by transport.

Food

We stongly suggest you to take care of food by yourselves as well. There will be campfire spots available to cook your food (with your own tools).

Rules

If you choose to participate in the event, you agree to a few simple rules which can be found on this site. You will be removed from the festival for non-compliance without any further explanation.

Refund

If it happens that the event will be canceled because of COVID-19 or other force majaure reasons - tickets will be refunded and money will be sent to the same account from which they were purchased.

Other info

The profit will be used to pay the artists and other contributors of the festival. For more details contact us at prieblanda@protonmail.com.
