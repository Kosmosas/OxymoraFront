RULES

By purchasing a ticket or making a reservation you agree to follow these rules:

  1. Swimming while drunk or under the influence of other drugs is not allowed, epecially at night time. In the worst case, you have to bring a sober friend with you. There will be no lifeguards. YOU YOURSELF ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR YOUR OWN SAFETY.
  2. Making a campfire at a spot not intended for that is forbidden. There will be a lot of campfire spots available, you will definitely be able to find one.
    1. If a camfire is not lit and you choose to do that, you are responsible for it automatically - you have to extinguish it before you leave.
  3. Use garbage cans (there will be plenty)
  4. Be kind to your fellow participants (as well as yourself). Aggressive, hateful or foolish behavior will not be tolerated, you will be removed from the festival territory without further explanation.
  5. No partying at the parking area. Misbehavior will be reported by the parking site owner on duty. There will be enough space inside the festival territory.
  6. No glass containers (bottles, jars, etc). Use plastic containers instead.

Not following these rules may lead to your removal from festival area. THANK YOU.
